About SorAI-gen

Welcome to SorAI-gen, where we're pioneering the frontier of Scripted Reality: When Language Becomes a Camera. Our mission is to revolutionize the way stories are told through the power of text-to-video AI and generative storytelling.

Our Vision

At SorAI-gen, we envision a world where the boundaries between written word and visual storytelling blur into a seamless cinematic synthesis. Inspired by groundbreaking technologies like Sora AI, we're crafting tools that transform text into vivid, dynamic video content.

What We Do

  • Develop cutting-edge text-to-video AI algorithms
  • Create intuitive platforms for generative storytelling
  • Push the boundaries of cinematic synthesis
  • Collaborate with storytellers, filmmakers, and technologists

Our Team

Behind SorAI-gen is a diverse team of AI researchers, creative writers, and visual artists. We're united by our passion for storytelling and our belief in the transformative power of AI in creative industries.
